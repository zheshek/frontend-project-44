## ��� Доступные игры (6 игр)

### 1. Brain Games (\`brain-games\`)
Простое приветствие и проверка установки.

\`\`\`bash
$ brain-games
Welcome to the Brain Games!
May I have your name? Anna
Hello, Anna!
\`\`\`

### 2. Brain Even (\`brain-even\`)
Проверка на чётность.

\`\`\`bash
$ brain-even
Welcome to the Brain Games!
May I have your name? Max
Answer "yes" if the number is even, otherwise answer "no".
Question: 15
Your answer: no
Correct!
Question: 6
Your answer: yes
Correct!
Question: 7
Your answer: no
Correct!
Congratulations, Max!
\`\`\`

### 3. Brain Calc (\`brain-calc\`)
Калькулятор (операции: +, -, *).

\`\`\`bash
$ brain-calc
Welcome to the Brain Games!
May I have your name? Sam
What is the result of the expression?
Question: 4 + 10
Your answer: 14
Correct!
Question: 25 - 11
Your answer: 14
Correct!
Question: 25 * 7
Your answer: 175
Correct!
Congratulations, Sam!
\`\`\`

### 4. Brain GCD (\`brain-gcd\`)
Наибольший общий делитель.

\`\`\`bash
$ brain-gcd
Welcome to the Brain Games!
May I have your name? Alex
Find the greatest common divisor of given numbers.
Question: 25 50
Your answer: 25
Correct!
Question: 100 52
Your answer: 4
Correct!
Question: 3 9
Your answer: 3
Correct!
Congratulations, Alex!
\`\`\`

### 5. Brain Progression (\`brain-progression\`)
Арифметическая прогрессия.

\`\`\`bash
$ brain-progression
Welcome to the Brain Games!
May I have your name? Lisa
What number is missing in the progression?
Question: 5 7 9 11 13 .. 17 19 21 23
Your answer: 15
Correct!
Question: 2 5 8 .. 14 17 20 23 26 29
Your answer: 11
Correct!
Question: 14 19 24 29 34 39 44 49 54 ..
Your answer: 59
Correct!
Congratulations, Lisa!
\`\`\`

### 6. Brain Prime (\`brain-prime\`)
Простое ли число?

**Победа:**
\`\`\`bash
$ brain-prime
Welcome to the Brain Games!
May I have your name? Sam
Answer "yes" if given number is prime. Otherwise answer "no".
Question: 7
Your answer: yes
Correct!
Question: 15
Your answer: no
Correct!
Question: 23
Your answer: yes
Correct!
Congratulations, Sam!
\`\`\`
