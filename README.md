## Доступные игры

### Brain Games (`brain-games`)
Простое приветствие.

```bash
$ brain-games
Welcome to the Brain Games!
May I have your name? Anna
Hello, Anna!
```

### Brain Even (`brain-even`)
Проверка на чётность.

```bash
$ brain-even
Welcome to the Brain Games!
May I have your name? Max
Answer "yes" if the number is even, otherwise answer "no".
Question: 15
Your answer: no
Correct!
Question: 6
Your answer: yes
Correct!
Question: 7
Your answer: no
Correct!
Congratulations, Max!
```

### Brain Calc (`brain-calc`)
Калькулятор.

```bash
$ brain-calc
Welcome to the Brain Games!
May I have your name? Sam
What is the result of the expression?
Question: 4 + 10
Your answer: 14
Correct!
Question: 25 - 11
Your answer: 14
Correct!
Question: 25 * 7
Your answer: 175
Correct!
Congratulations, Sam!
```

### Brain GCD (`brain-gcd`)
Наибольший общий делитель.

**Победа:**
```bash
$ brain-gcd
Welcome to the Brain Games!
May I have your name? Alex
Find the greatest common divisor of given numbers.
Question: 25 50
Your answer: 25
Correct!
Question: 100 52
Your answer: 4
Correct!
Question: 3 9
Your answer: 3
Correct!
Congratulations, Alex!
```

**Поражение:**
```bash
Question: 25 50
Your answer: 1
'1' is wrong answer ;(. Correct answer was '25'.
Let's try again, Alex!
```
